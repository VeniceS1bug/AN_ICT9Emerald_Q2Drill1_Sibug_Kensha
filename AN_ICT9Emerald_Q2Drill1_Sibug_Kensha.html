EMERGENCY GO BAG ESSENTIALS

Emergency Food and Water

Keep at least one liter of clean water for hydration during emergencies. Pack non-perishable food items such as energy bars and canned goods.

First Aid Kit

Include basic medical supplies like bandages, antiseptic, and pain relievers.

Flashlight & Whistle

Use a reliable flashlight to see clearly during power outages or at night. Use it to signal for help and make rescuers aware of your location.

Extra Clothes

Bring a change of clothes to stay warm and dry during an evacuation.
